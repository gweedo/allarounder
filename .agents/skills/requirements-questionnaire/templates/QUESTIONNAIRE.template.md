# [PROJECT NAME] — Stakeholder Questionnaire

**Purpose:** gather the product/content/business details needed to write the PRD and finalize the design.

**How to answer:** write under each question — short notes or bullets are fine. If a question doesn't apply, write "not applicable". You can fill this in together.

---

## 1. The product / offering

1.1 In one sentence, what is [PROJECT NAME]?

1.2 What are the main topics / features / offerings?

1.3 What is the mission — what should the user walk away with?

1.4 What makes it different from the alternatives? Why choose it?

---

## 2. Format / production

2.1 What form does the content/product take?

2.2 How often is it produced/updated?

2.3 Is anything already live? Links?

---

## 3. The audience

3.1 Who is the typical user? (who, where, context)

3.2 Generalist or a specific niche? Which?

3.3 What language(s) do they use?

3.4 How do they discover products/content like this?

3.5 What similar things do they already use? (examples)

---

## 4. Goals

4.1 What is the #1 thing this should achieve? (rank if several)

4.2 What does success look like in 6–12 months?

4.3 What should a visitor do first?

---

## 5. Scope & priorities

5.1 What must be in the first version?

5.2 What can wait until later?

5.3 Any hard constraints (budget, tools, brand)?

---

## 6. Tone, brand, timeline

6.1 What tone/voice fits? (formal, casual, playful…)

6.2 Brand status — name, logo, colors? When ready?

6.3 Target launch date or deadline?

6.4 Anything important we didn't ask?

---

*Thank you — these answers go straight into the PRD and the design.*
